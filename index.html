Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many classes will children get to attend upon enrollment?

    Each program (Character through Culture & Character through Contemporary Approach) is taught at 3 levels per year.
    Each Level consists of 15 Sessions which are delivered weekly. After 15 weeks, you can continue to the next level. Content of each level is different.

  • How many students will be learning with my child in each class?

    We take maximum 6 Students in a class

  • Are Personal Sessions available?

    Yes. You may opt for 1 to 1 sessions as well.

  • What if my child misses a class due to sickness or travel?

    Children are allowed to miss max of 2 classes per quarter; children can attend sessions of other batches to cover missed classes. Contact your Program educator to find dates and times of other programs.

  • Who will teach my child?

    All programs of Val-Ed are delivered by trained and approved educators who undergo several rounds of screening, training & demo before teaching your child.

  • Will educators change?

    While Educators are devoted to a certain batch and take them throughout the level; In rare occasions, due to emergencies, educators can be replaced with another competent educator, in the rare event of an educator change, you will be updated proactively.

  • Will course completion certificate be provided?

    Yes, Val-Ed issues certificate for course once you complete it.

  • Will you extend the timing of the classes?

    No, the modules designed by Val-Ed will take not more than the said time to complete your courses. You will not be asked for your extra time for course completion.

  • Will there be any sort of assessment?

    Val-Ed regularly tracks child’s progress through weekly feedback, mid & end of program activity & observational assessment. Same is made available to parents at the end of the program.
