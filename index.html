Why Character?

  • Character is the "X factor" that parenting experts & top educationists have deemed integral to success, both in school & in life

  • Parents don’t always have the time or expertise, Schools might have it but focus more on academic achievement

  • Children who undergo quality character education report higher academic performance, levels of positivity & fewer disciplinary issues
