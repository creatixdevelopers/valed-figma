Cultural Approach

Saksham

Val-Ed offers a fun, exciting & modern approach to building Cultural Intelligence in Children that helps them not only understand Indian culture but also live life with meaning, happiness & success.

Designed exclusively for Indian children living outside of India, Saksham is a non-religious, non-political & non-preachy approach where children learn with trained educators, customized content & a personal touch

What will children learn

How do children learn

Children can experience upto 3 levels of Cultural Intelligence each year. Each Level consists of 15 sessions that are conducted once a week.
